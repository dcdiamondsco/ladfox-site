Common questions before enquiring

A few quick answers for people who are still deciding whether to get in touch.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. Many clients start with only a few saved images or a general idea. We can help shape the design from there.

Do you offer lab grown and natural diamonds?

Yes. We can guide you through both options clearly so you can choose the right fit for your priorities and budget.

Is bespoke only for very high budgets?

Not at all. Bespoke can work across a range of budgets. Sharing your budget early helps us guide you more accurately.

Do you work with clients outside London?

Yes. We work with clients across the UK and internationally, with insured delivery included.
